Our Process

How We build Websites in 5 percise steps.

At CodeGrambha, we follow a proven, results-driven workflow that blends strategy, creativity, and technology. Every project is delivered with precision, performance, and purpose.

step-1

Discover

We begin by understanding your goals, target audience, and unique brand. Through a detailed consultation, we gather everything we need to align the website with your vision.

step-2

Plan

Based on your needs, we structure the website's flow, content hierarchy, and features. This ensures the design and user experience will match your goals.

step-3

Design

Our UI/UX team turns strategy into visuals with a clean, modern, and interactive interface always keeping the user in focus.

step-4

Development

Using clean, scalable code, we bring your website to life. Whether it's a personal site, e-commerce platform, or custom solution — we ensure performance, security, and SEO readiness.

step-5

Launch & Support

We launch your site with full testing, optimize for search and speed, and offer ongoing support to keep your site updated and performing.
